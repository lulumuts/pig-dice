PIG DICE

RULES: Two players roll the dice. Player 1 plays first and their total is added up until he reaches a total of 100.

  • If either player rolls 1, there turn is over and they score nothing.
  • If two 1's are rolled, the player's entire score is lost, and the turn ends.
  • If a double is rolled, the point total is added to the turn total, but the player MUST roll again.
